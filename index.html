About Mathmataz

Mathmataz is a mental math game where you must keep a running total as numbers are added, subtracted, multiplied and divided. The game 'Up or Down' is strictly addition and subtraction. The game 'The Big Four' involves addition and subtraction as well as multiplication and division. You can choose from 5 levels of difficulty and change the speed at which the game is played. After each round, you are given an opportunity to review the last game. You can also visit the Stats page to see how many games you've won or lost, your Best Game, and your performance in the last 10 rounds. Improve your mental math abilities by playing Mathmataz, and don't forget to have fun!

Privacy Policy

This is the privacy policy for Mathmataz, which is a mental math game created by Christopher Ganey. This application collects no information about you, your browser, or your viewing device, nor does it transmit any information to other applications or web sites. If enabled by your browser, this application will save your game statistics and settings to your browser's local storage, and it will retrieve that information the next time you play the game. This information can be removed from your browser's local storage by clearing your browsing data. When you visit www.mathmataz.com, the game is loaded into your browser. You can continue to play the game whether you are online or offline until you close the browser window. If you have any questions about this application, please send an email to chrisganeymedia@gmail.com.
